BIO

Zevensuy Rodriguez is a prototyper and designer born in Miami, FL. He graduated from Univ. of Miami’s Visual Communications Undergraduate program and has a masters from NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP). His research focuses on user’s interaction with internet objects, by creating interesting interfaces. He also explores combining traditional fabrication techniques with digital ones.
